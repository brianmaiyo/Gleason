Our History

Gleason Construction Ltd was established in 2022 in Kisumu, Kenya, with a vision to transform the construction landscape in East Africa. Founded by a team of experienced engineers and construction professionals, our company quickly established itself as a reliable partner for quality construction services.

Since our incorporation, we have successfully completed numerous projects across Kenya, including residential buildings, commercial spaces, and infrastructure developments. Our growth has been driven by our commitment to excellence, integrity, and client satisfaction.

2022

Company Founding

Gleason Construction Ltd was officially incorporated in Kisumu, Kenya, bringing together a team of skilled professionals with decades of combined experience in the construction industry.

2023

Expansion of Services

We expanded our service offerings to include architectural design, structural analysis, and specialized construction services to meet the growing demands of our clients.

2024

Regional Growth

Our operations expanded beyond Kisumu to other major cities in Kenya, including Nakuru, Eldoret, and surrounding areas, establishing Gleason Construction as a regional leader.

2025

Today

Today, Gleason Construction Ltd continues to grow and innovate, maintaining our commitment to delivering world-class construction services while embracing sustainable building practices and cutting-edge technologies.

Our Core Values

At Gleason Construction, our work is guided by a set of core values that define who we are and how we operate. These principles are the foundation of our company culture and drive our commitment to excellence.

Safety

We prioritize the safety of our team, clients, and the public above all else. Our rigorous safety protocols and continuous training ensure that every project is completed without compromising well-being.

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices. Our clients trust us because we deliver on our promises and maintain the highest standards of professional conduct.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies, methods, and ideas to continuously improve our services. By staying at the forefront of industry developments, we deliver innovative solutions that exceed expectations.

Teamwork

We believe in the power of collaboration and mutual respect. Our diverse team works together seamlessly, combining their expertise to achieve common goals and deliver exceptional results.

Sustainability

We are committed to environmentally responsible construction practices. By minimizing our ecological footprint and promoting sustainable building methods, we contribute to a healthier planet for future generations.

Our values aren't just words—they're the principles that guide every decision we make and every project we undertake.
